Streetwear

Casual clothing of a style worn especially by members of various urban youth subcultures.

Steampunk

Science fiction that has a historical setting and typically features steam-powered machinery rather than advanced technology

Classic

Classics are those styles that you don't even have to think about. You just know they will be acceptable from one year to the next.

Vintage

Styles and trends of the era they represent.
